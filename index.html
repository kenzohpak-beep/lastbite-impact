LastBite helps restaurants, cafés, and bakeries sell end-of-day surplus for 40–70% off. Customers get affordable meals for pickup or delivery, and perfectly good food avoids the bin.

What you get with LastBite

Fast, simple, and focused on surplus — not full menus.

  • Guaranteed discounts: typically 40–70% off.
  • Clear pickup windows: timed around partner closing hours.
  • Filters that matter: vegetarian options, nut-free notes, and more.
  • Impact tracking: estimated food and CO₂ saved per order.
  • Social enterprise: 5% of profits donated to the UN World Food Programme (WFP).
